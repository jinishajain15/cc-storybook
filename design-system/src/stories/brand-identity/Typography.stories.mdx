        # Typography
    </div>

    Typography guidelines are crucial to ensure clear hierarchies are established and that information is organized appropriately based on its importance.
    When Typography is applied well within design it enables content to be communicated clearly, effectively, and efficiently.

    The primary typeface for all TruShield Insurance consumer facing communications is **Freight Sans Pro** or **Freight Micro Pro**.
    Chosen for its simplicity, approachability and compatibility with the Corporate Marks.
    **Freight Sans Pro** or **Freight Micro Pro** also boasts a wide variety weights (e.g. Light, Regular, Bold, Extra Bold etc.), allowing
    for great flexibility when constructing informational hierarchies.
    The consistent application of TruShield Corporate Typography, in conjunction with the TruShield logo and Colours, creates a
    unique typographic style which is the visual property of TruShield Insurance. It makes all communications instantly recognizable, differentiating TruShield from our competition.
